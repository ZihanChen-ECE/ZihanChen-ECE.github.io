Thesis Projects

Cardiovascular Simulation Toolkit

A non-invasive assessment of aortic coarctation severity using computational fluid dynamics

Aortic coarctation (CoA) causes pressure drop across the narrowing. Traditional assessment of CoA severity is measurment of peressure drop by catheterization, with a 1 in a 1000 risk of patient death or serious injury.

Our project provides a cardiovascular MRI (CMR) based pressure drop calculating toolkit. By implementing patient specific conditions, the computational fluid dynamics (CFD) solver could provide a non-invasive diagnose of CoA severity with high accuracy.
